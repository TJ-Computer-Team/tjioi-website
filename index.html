Logistics

The contest will be held in-person and online on Saturday, May 17.


The in-person contest will be at the Thomas Jefferson High School for Science and Technology, open to anyone. Registration is first come first serve and each team not from TJHSST must be registered with an adult sponsor or chaperone. The team sponsor must stay in the building for whole contest time and may proctor the contest. Lunch and snacks will be provided.



Registration for the online version will open soon.


If you have any questions or concerns, email us at tjioiofficers@gmail.com.

Code

  • For the in-person contest, our grader will support C++17, Java 17.0.14, and Python 3.11.2. The information on how to access the grading website/online judge will be provided the day of the contest.
  • All input will be read through standard input, and output should be done through standard output.

Contest

  • Problems will range from USACO Bronze level to USACO Platinum level.
  • Rankings will be determined by the number of problems solved. Tiebreakers will be broken by penalties (incorrect submissions and time submitted).
  • In-person teams should use a desktop we provide.
  • Communication between non-team members and unauthorized use of the internet will result in immediate disqualification.
  • Students are allowed to bring written reference material, paper, and writing utensils.

Prizes

The top pre-college teams attending in-person in each division (High School, Middle School, TJ Students) will receive gift cards totalling for over $500 and a certificate.
